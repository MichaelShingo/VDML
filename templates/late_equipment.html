Late Equipment Email Generator

  1. When a user is late, this function generates the email that you can send to the user to remind them to bring back the equipment. We have been waiting until they are 2 days past the return time to send them this personal email.
  2. Log into connect2 and go to the Dashboard.
  3. Select the late booking from the Check ins.
  4. Click “Proceed to check in”.
  5. Click on the Con number (this takes you to the “Resource booking summary" page).
  6. Hit cmd + a to copy all of the text on the page.
  7. Paste the copied text into the text box in the VDML app and hit the “Submit” button.
  8. ** Currently, text from connect2 must be copied from Google Chrome or Safari. (Firefox does not provide the correct formatting).
